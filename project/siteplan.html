LookGood – Fashion & Lifestyle Inspiration

Website Planning Document

Site Name

LookGood – A brand focused on empowering individuals to look and feel their best through style and self-care tips. The name is short, memorable, and matches my existing brand identity.

Optional domain availability: lookgood.com

Site Purpose

The LookGood website will be a hub for style inspiration, outfit ideas, grooming tips, and lifestyle advice. It will serve young adults and professionals seeking to improve their personal presentation and confidence.

Scenarios

  • How can I put together affordable but stylish outfits for work?
  • What grooming tips will help me look professional and polished?
  • How can I order LookGood clothing from the website?

Color Scheme

Gold (#f9c74f) – Used for headings, highlights, and accents.
Black (#1a1a1a) – Used for header/footer backgrounds and text emphasis.
White (#ffffff) – Used for main page backgrounds and to provide clean contrast for text.

Typography

  • Playfair Display – Used for main headings (H1, H2).
  • Montserrat – Used for body text, navigation, and smaller headings.
